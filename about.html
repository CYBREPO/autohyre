About Autohyre

is a car sharing/hiring marketplace where you can book any car you want, wherever you want it, from a vibrant community of trusted hosts across the major cities in Nigeria and it suits all kinds of occasion and budgets.

Our services

Autohyre brings you a new cutting-edge experience in car hire service in Nigeria with affordable prices and relishable experiences. With every trip you take with Autohyre we will make you thirst for more.

Sustainable car travel on Turo

Turo offsets 100% of our estimated global carbon emissions by investing in greenhouse gas-reducing projects, so you can rest easy building your businesses and satisfying your wanderlust knowing that our carbon footprint is accounted for.
